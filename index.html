New Thought Movement

This is the school of thought which seeks to align you with your higher self via several methods including affirmation, meditation and self-realisation.

Founding Fathers of The New Thought Movement

Neville Lancelot Goddard

(Feb 19, 1905 - Oct 1, 1972),
generally known as Neville, was a Barbadian author who wrote on the Bible, mysticism, and self-help.

Joseph Denis Murphy

(May 20, 1898 - Dec 16, 1981)
was an Irish author and New Thought minister, ordained in Divine Science and Religious Science.

Emmet Fox

(30 Jul 1886 - 13 Aug 1951)
was an Irish New Thought spiritual leader of the early 20th century, primarily through years of the Great Depression, until his death in 1951.

Florence Scovel Shinn

(Sept 24, 1871 - Oct 17, 1940)
was an American artist and book illustrator who became a New Thought spiritual teacher and metaphysical writer in her middle years.
